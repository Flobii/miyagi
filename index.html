The Miyagi Aiming Method

The Range

Step One

Start off by going to the Range. Set your Bots to Easy and give them Armor. Suit yourself with a Sheriff and infinite Ammunition.

Your goal in this Part is not to miss a Shot. You should focus on aiming at the Head and in Easy Mode there is pleanty of Time for you to hit them without getting stressed!

Later on you can incorporate Movement into your Training. Discipline your Aim and do not hurry on shooting.

Step Two

The next Step is using Medium Speed for the Bots. As before your Goal is to hit their Head and not hurrying.

There is no Problem if you miss a couple of Shots!

Ensure that you are aiming for the Head only, not going for the Body or Legs.

Step Three

Now for the last Part in the Range. Set your Bots on 50 Streak with Strafes on. Also upgrade your Sheriff to a Guardian.

Your Goal here is to track the Bots Movement a little bit before shooting for the Head.

After the first 10-25 Bots you can stop and go your on Way. I personally like tracking all 50 of them.

After some time you'll find a Rythm. If you need a harder Challenge, try setting the Bots to fast instead!

Deathmatch

Step One

For the first few Minutes of the Deathmatch your only Goal is to track the Enemies without shooting yet. You want to observe the Movement.

Surely you will die a lot, but remember, you're not here to win, your Aim is to to track heads.

Step Two

After tracking your Enemies for some time you can start going for Sheriff Kills.

After some more Time, when you feel comfortable and have your Aim disciplined you can start going for Guardian Kills.

Step Three

After some more Time, when you're feeling comfortable with the Guardian, you can finally switch to the Vandal.

But still don't forget your Target. Deathmatch is not a Competition, you get Nothing from finishing first.
